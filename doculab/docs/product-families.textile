p(note). *Product Families are completely optional.* They are now little more than a logical grouping of products. Coupons and components are now directly attached to products.

h3. What is a Product Family?

*Product Families* provide an optional, logical grouping for your Products.


h3. Creating Product Families

Product Families are created via your "Site":/sites Dashboard from the "Products" tab.  Clicking the "Create New Product Family" will take you where you want to go.
